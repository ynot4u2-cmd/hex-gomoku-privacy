Abstract Strategy • Mobile Board Game

Hex Gomoku

Hex Gomoku is a polished strategy board game built for players who enjoy tactical depth, clean presentation, and meaningful long-term progression. It combines local multiplayer, AI opponents, Elo-based profiles, customizable game settings, and optional training tools in a mobile-first experience designed for both quick sessions and more serious play.

Core Features

Hex-Based Tactical Gameplay

A clean abstract strategy experience focused on board control, pattern recognition, spatial planning, and tactical counterplay instead of luck-driven mechanics.

Local Multiplayer and Solo Play

Supports human-vs-human matches on one device, human-vs-AI games, and AI-vs-AI setups for experimentation, observation, and balance tuning.

Multiple AI Skill Levels

Includes several AI tiers ranging from entry-level training opponents up to stronger premium challenge levels for advanced players.

Elo-Based Player Profiles

Human players can use named profiles with Elo ratings, rating deltas, win/loss stats, draw tracking, and match history for long-term progression.

Move Suggestions and Study Tools

Optional move suggestions help players review positions, learn better decisions, and turn regular matches into a more educational training experience.

Flexible Board Sizes

Different board radii support both faster tactical sessions and broader strategic battles, with larger boards available on supported devices.

Full Match Control

Undo, redo, move logging, game sharing, and status feedback make it easy to analyze decisions, revisit lines, and continue learning after each match.

Theme and Interface Options

A polished mobile UI with theme settings, responsive layout behavior, and touch-first controls keeps the experience readable and comfortable during long sessions.

Premium Expansion Path

Premium expands the game with stronger AI opponents, move suggestions, ad-free play, and additional board-size access on supported devices.

Why It Stands Out

Hex Gomoku is not built around random rewards or noisy arcade loops. Its strength is a thoughtful blend of elegant strategy mechanics and player-friendly usability.

  • Quick to understand, but deep enough for repeated competitive play.
  • Suitable for both casual offline matches and deliberate skill improvement.
  • Built-in progression gives repeated play a sense of continuity and mastery.
  • AI and training features support both experimentation and structured learning.
